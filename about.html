Eli Ramirez Chicago-based Brain Without a Cause

Life has taken me many places: to the international stage for Barbershop singing and winning gold, to consulting with dozens of hospitality-focused businesses all thoughout the U.S., to living in a cabin in the woods at a sleep-away camp for over 100 kids, to competing in a national barista competiton twice. I love forming my life around moving and learning from everyone and everything I can. This website is a space for me to talk about the most memorable things I've experienced week by week, and a place for me to get all the crazy ideas I have out of my head. Want to come along with me and join in on the fun? I have a weekly newsletter! And a podcast!
