Eli Ramirez Chicago-based Brain Without a Cause

I try to live my life needing as little as possible.

Getting stopped cold from a work injury in early 2019, I realized that my life was quickly moving forward and I was not in control. Having this unexpected time to reflect, I figured out that the source of my unhappiness was my own inability to create and foster deep connections with the people and places around me. This website was born to get me back in the driver's seat of my own life. I am learning how to intentionally contribute to relationships, how to express thoughts and feelings through writing, and how to create a loop of accountability with myself and anyone reading this.

Needing little is one thing, but truly knowing what you rely on for survival is another. I am motivated by being able to learn new skills and by spending time with people that push me out of my comfort zone. I do not need a job or money to find those two things, and the realization that my own mind is enough to keep me going is liberating beyond belief.

If you leave this page pondering one thing, here's a good place to start: "Your head was made for creating ideas, not for storing them."
